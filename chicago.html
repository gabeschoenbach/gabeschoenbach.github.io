# live performance

Theater, comedy, jazz. Below are some great venues.

  • The Annoyance. Lakeview East, off of the Belmont Red Line stop. Good, weird improv comedy.
  • iO Theater. Off of the North/Clybourn Red Line stop. Good improv comedy, tends to be less weird than the Annoyance.
  • Neo-Futurists. Andersonville. Experimental Theater.
  • Court Theater. Hyde Park.
  • Jazz Showcase. South Loop. My favorite jazz club. Great couches.
  • Kingston Mines. Lincoln Park. Very good blues club, high energy. Go after midnight!

Below are some particular good groups/shows.

  • My favorite independent improv comedy groups: Bridal Darty, Super Human, Swim Test, Peggy, Deep Schwa.
  • Improvised Shakespeare. Improv comedy in couplets.
  • The Infinite Wrench. 30 plays in 60 minutes.

# food

Woefully incomplete list of some of my favorite restaurants around the city.

  • Asia on Argyle. Uptown. Tons of great (mostly Vietnamese) restaurants. Especially recommend a banh mi from Ba Le and pho from Tank Noodle.
  • Green Street Smoked Meats. West Loop. BBQ.
  • Lao Peng You. West Town. Unbelievable Chinese food.
  • Valois. Hyde Park (53rd street). Cash-only. The steak omelette absolutely rips.
  • Taqueria Moran. Logan Square, off of the California Blue Line stop. Best tacos in the city.
  • Demera. Uptown. Great Ethiopian food.
  • Fiya. Andersonville. Very good middle-eastern restaurant.
  • The Gundis. Lakeview. Fantastic Kurdish food, especially good for brunch.
  • Cafecito. Locations in the Loop, South Loop, Gold Coast, Lincoln Park. Very good Cuban food.
  • North Buena Deli & Wine. Buena Park. Good deli sandwiches for under $10!
  • m henry. Andersonville. Great American brunch.
  • Allende. Lincoln Park. Very good tacos. Open late so go post-Kingston Mines.

# activities

Even more woefully-incomplete list of fun things to do around the city…

  • Graceland Cemetery. Wrigleyville. Try to spot a coyote.
  • Brick of Chicago. Architectural walking tour. Choose your neighborhood!
  • Andersonville Wine Walk. They somehow pick the two most beautiful weekends of the year to do this.
