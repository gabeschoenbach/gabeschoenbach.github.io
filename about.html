# quick stuff

I grew up in Glen Ellen, California, in the heart of Sonoma Valley. I like to rock climb and run on the Chicago lakefront, though I miss exploring the mountains back home. I come from a family of musicians and enjoy playing the piano and the electric bass. I’m obsessed with making the dumbest possible jokes, as frequently as my friends will allow.

I’m a first-year PhD student in the Computer Science department at the University of Chicago, advised by Aloni Cohen. I’m interested in using theoretical computer science to tackle problems in civic life, with an eye towards legal and policy applications.
